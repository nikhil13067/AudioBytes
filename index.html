Welcome To AudioBytes!

Audiobytes Goals & Objectives

  • To encourage Music and Cultural activity in the institution, and to create an environment of appreciation of the different fields of Music.
  • To give a platform to people to display their talents in the respective subdivisions under Music.
  • To organize inter-college and intra-college events and competitions for an engaging role in Music from the institute.
  • To maintain the institute's Music equipment and organize & maintain a system to monitor them for free usage for every institute member.
